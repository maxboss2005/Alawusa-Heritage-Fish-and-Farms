About Alawusa Heritage

Over 40 years of excellence in Nigerian agriculture

A Legacy of Over 4 Decades in Nigerian Agriculture

Alawusa Heritage Fish & Farms stands as a testament to enduring dedication and innovation in Nigeria's agricultural sector. Founded over 4 decades ago by our visionary matriarch, Mrs. Deborah Zuntemen, the business began with a singular commitment to providing quality protein to our community. Born from humble beginnings, it quickly grew into a trusted name, deeply woven into the fabric of local food supply chains.

Our Vision

Leadership & Vision for the Future

Today, the legacy is passionately carried forward by Mr. Viwakennu Elijah Sewanu, who serves as the CEO/MD. While the business retains its foundational family values, it is now driven by a strategic vision for modernization and expansion. We aim to become Nigeria's leading provider of sustainable, high-quality fish and farm products while maintaining our commitment to community nourishment.

Our Team

Professional & Experienced

Our operations at Alawusa Heritage Fish & Farms are driven by a professional and experienced team of 10 staff. Their collective expertise spans farming, processing, sales, and distribution, ensuring efficiency and quality across all facets. This includes individuals whose long-standing involvement contributes significantly to our established heritage. A dedicated Logistics Manager further streamlines our sourcing and delivery processes.

Sustainability

Commitment to Quality & Food Security

We specialize in integrated farming of fish, poultry, pigs, and fresh eggs, maintaining rigorous standards for quality and sustainability. Beyond our own farm-fresh produce, we proudly source and retail a diverse range of popular seafood. Our signature uniquely smoked fish offers delicious value, bringing the best of our farm directly to Nigerian homes and markets. Based in Ipokia, Ogun State, Alawusa Heritage Fish & Farms is dedicated to enhancing food security across Lagos and Ogun food market and beyond.
